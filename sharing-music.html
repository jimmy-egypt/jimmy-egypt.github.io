Sharing Music

Wed 23 September 2020
music sharing

Intent: Sharing music to understand each others influences and fascinations

Either with two headphones and a splitter, or a set of good speakers, take some time to exchange music. Have it comfortable loud and take the time to listen to the full song. Then ask why the other has chosen to play this song and what is inspiring about it. Switch every song.
