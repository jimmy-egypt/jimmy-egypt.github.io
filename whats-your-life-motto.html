What's your life motto?

Wed 23 September 2020
writing storytelling sharing question

Intent: Practicing honesty and openness

Ask yourself or someone else the question: What's your life motto? Or your values to live by? Take a piece of paper and a couple minutes to write down what comes to your mind. Then, ask the follow-up question: In what way are you not living by that motto? Or: What was the last time you didn't live by that motto? Again, take your time to write down your thoughts, your story, or just tell them to the other(s).

Drawn from the practice of Brandon Stanton.
