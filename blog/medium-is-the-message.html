Medium is the message

Wed 23 September 2020
sense making writing sharing

Intent: Considering the different types of media to experience a message

If you have your mission statement, and know what you would like to bring across, what kind of medium would be suiting to deliver your message? Take a few minutes to consider the different channels through which a story can be experienced. What brought you to believe in that in the first place? Write that down and share with the others.
