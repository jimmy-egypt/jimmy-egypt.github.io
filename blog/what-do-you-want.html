What do you want?

Wed 23 September 2020
sense making sharing outcome process question writing warm-up

Intent: Taking time to consider desires

Ask yourself or someone else the question: What do you want? Take a piece of paper and 10 minutes to write down what comes to your mind. This question/exercise is good for the very beginning of a process of expression, as it can help to formulate desires. Take the Wunderfrage as a follow-up question.
