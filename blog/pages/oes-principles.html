OES Principles

What if there was a public service that supports people in experiencing meaningful moments of creative expression?

Open Expression Support is an experimental project, starting individual trajectories with members of the public to come to an experience of expression. To experiment together and provide support to reach a creative mode. To engage in a meaningful experience and to hold space for each other to discover something new about ourselves.

Having experience in some fields more than others, the goal is to consult and include other practitioners (peers) with particular strengths in the process. For the time being, I envision the number of personal, one-on-one sessions to be once a week (1,5h?) for 8 consecutive weeks, aiming for 6-10 participants in the first round of experiments. Open Expression Support is free and executed in public, for the public.
