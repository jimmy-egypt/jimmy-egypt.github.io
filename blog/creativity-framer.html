Creativity Framer

Wed 23 September 2020
sense making writing sharing warm-up

Intent: Registering what someone already possesses

Draw out the above on an A4. In the first column, write down 3-5 areas/activities in which you can easily be creative (where you can feel the creative energy flow). Next to the first column, write down which positive personal attributes are associated with that activity. In the top row, write down areas/activities where you have a hard time being creative.

Drawn from the practice of George Parker.
