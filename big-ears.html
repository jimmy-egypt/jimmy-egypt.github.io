Big ears

Tue 23 June 2020
By David Martens
Category: exercises

INTENTION: Taking elements from a soundscape as impulse(s) for bodily movement, and back.

When inside a room, position yourself in front of an open window. Listen to what is there. What do you observe, what are you hearing? Is there a specific sound or noise which gets your attention? Try to take in that sound by producing the same tone vocally, but softly. What happens if you use that sound as an impulse to guide your bodily movement? How to embody the acoustic quality of the sound you picked out of the street soundscape?

Take your bodily interpretation inside the space and listen out again to the new sounds that come to you when moving yourself. Can you integrate those sounds into your movement as well? -15 minutes
