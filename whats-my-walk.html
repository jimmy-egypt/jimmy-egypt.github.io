What's my walk?

Wed 23 September 2020
movement bodily awareness walking posture proprioception

Intent: Perceiving the way you, and people around you, walk

Free yourself of any bags or anything your carrying. Now just walk. Start off with thinking how your mother walks. Or your father or brother. How do they walk and can you imitate that? Once you think you have it, you can really exaggerate, making your walks sillier and sillier. Now get back to you. How do you walk when you are running late? How do you walk when you are on holiday? How are the shoulders? What's with your arms?
