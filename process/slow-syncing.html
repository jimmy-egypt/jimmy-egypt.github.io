Slow syncing

Tue 23 June 2020
movement improv slowness connection bodily awareness

INTENTION: Using slowness as a device to connect through movement.

Start with finding your own slowness in movement. What happens with the positioning of your body? Make eye contact with someone in the room and allow yourself to sync to their movement. If you think you are going slow, go even slower. How slow can you go? And does slowness make it easier to connect? 3-7 min
