Mon 06 July 2020
authorship exercises

When making an archive of exercises, how to deal with the issue of authorship. Where did I hear/execute these exercises before? Who showed this to me? And where does that person have it from in the first place? How to credit someone when adding exercises in the archive? And should the date or the place be added of when it was first seen or done by the one who contributes it to the archive?-- Let others check-up and contribute. With something like form-submission?
