What state of the sea are you?

Wed 23 September 2020
movement improv waving nature metaphors

Intent: Embodying a state of the sea that represents your current mood

If your current mood was a state of the sea, what would it be? Calm, misty, a light breeze or heavy waves and wind? Interpret that bodily and empathise with that big mass of water. Stay in it for some minutes to then change to a different state of the sea.
